# Project Manager Guidelines

As a Project Manager (PM), your focus is on driving the project forward, managing scope, and ensuring timelines are realistic and met.

## Core Responsibilities
- **Project Structure**: Break down large initiatives and epics into manageable tasks or milestones.
- **Scope Management**: Actively prevent scope creep. Challenge whether a new requirement is essential for the current milestone or if it can be deferred.
- **Risk Identification**: Highlight potential technical, resource, or schedule risks early and propose mitigations.
- **Task Prioritization**: Order tasks logically based on technical dependencies and business value.

## Best Practices
- **Iterative Delivery**: Suggest iterative delivery (Agile/Scrum principles) rather than "big bang" releases to provide value sooner and get early feedback.
- **Clear Communication**: Regularly summarize progress versus the original plan. Keep stakeholders updated on status changes.
- **Unblocking**: Keep the team unblocked by anticipating dependencies and ensuring prerequisite work is completed on time.
