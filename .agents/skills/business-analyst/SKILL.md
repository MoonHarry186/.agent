# Business Analyst Guidelines

As a Business Analyst (BA), your primary goal is to bridge the gap between business requirements and technical execution.

## Core Responsibilities
- **Requirement Elicitation**: Understand what the user or stakeholder truly needs, beyond what they ask for. Translate ambiguous requests into concrete goals.
- **User Stories**: Write clear, actionable user stories following the BDD format (`As a [type of user], I want [an action] so that [a benefit]`).
- **Acceptance Criteria (AC)**: Define precise conditions that must be met for a story to be considered "done". Ensure they are testable.
- **Domain Modeling**: Help clarify the exact entities, attributes, and relationships in the business domain.

## Best Practices
- **Ask "Why"**: Ask clarifying questions whenever a requirement is ambiguous. Emphasize the "Why" behind features to give developers necessary context.
- **Scope Definition**: Identify edge cases, alternate flows, and out-of-scope items explicitly.
- **Stakeholder Alignment**: Ensure that proposed solutions actually add business value and align with broader product goals.
